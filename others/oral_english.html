# 预约看病 (Making an Appointment)

Role A Role B
Good morning. This is Doctor Johnson's office.
What can I do for you?		 Yes, this is Mrs. Lee.
I'd like to make an appointment to see the doctor this week.
Ok, Mrs Lee.
What is the matter?		 I have a fever. （我好像发烧了）
Well, let's see.
He is fully booked on Monday and Tuesday.
Will Wednesday be OK for you, Mrs. Lee?		 I have to work on Wednesday.
Is Doctor Johnson available on Saturday?
I'm afraid the office is closed on weekends.
How about Friday afternoon?		 That sounds good!
I will come then.

# 发烧 (Having a Fever)

Role A Role B
Hello, Miss.
Sit down, please.		 Hi, I am felling sick.
Oh, what's wrong? I feel weak and have a sore throat （嗓子疼）.
Put this thermometer under your tongue first.
When did it start?		 Last night.
You do have a fever.
Let me take a look at your throat.
Open your mouth please.		 Is it serious?
I don't think so.
I am sure you will be better in no time.		 Do I need to take anything?
I will give you some medicine for it.
Be sure to take it as prescribed and drink plenty of water.		 I will.
I hope you will get well soon. Thank you.

# 失眠 (Insomnia)

Role A Role B
Good morning.
What seems to be the trouble?		 I have been suffering from insomnia for three months.
Do you get headaches? Yes, and I have no appetite.（没有胃口）
Let me take your blood pressure.
Don't worry.
You are just overworked and a little exhausted.		 What should I do?
Here is a prescription for you.
Try to keep your mind off your work.		 Thank you, doctor. I will.

# 复查病情 (Re-Examination)

Role A Role B
How are you doing, Mr. Smith? I am felling better now.
The medicine is really effective.
I am glad to hear that!
Let me look at the x-ray results.		 OK. Do I need more medicine, doctor?
I am afraid you have not fully recorvered yet.
I will prescribe some antibiotics（抗生素） for you.		 How long should I take these?
For one week. Are there any side effects?
No, but get plenty of sleep and keep away from alcohol. I will. When do I need to come back?
Come back and see me in about four days,
and we'll see if we need to change the dosage （剂量）.		 Thank you, Doctor.

# 头痛 (Headache)

Role A Role B
Good afternoon.
What seems to be the trouble?		 I've had a bad headache for two days.
Any other sympton?
Do you feel light-headed （头晕）?		 Yes, my nose is often running. （流鼻涕）
I see.
What the time of the day does your head hurt the most?		 Mostly in the evening.
Now let me take a look at your head.
It looks like you've got a chronic migraine（慢性偏头痛）
along with a cold.		 Is it serious, doctor?
No. I'll prescribe some medicine for you
and you should feel better in no time.		 Anything else should I pay attention to?
Yes. You should keep away from alcohol
and live a healthy lifestyle.		 Thank you. I'll keep that in mind.
